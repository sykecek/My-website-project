SUPERGIANTS

Supergiants are extremely massive and luminous stars. They can be blue or red, depending on temperature and evolutionary stage.

Because they burn fuel very quickly, supergiants live only millions of years (short compared to Sun-like stars). Many end their lives in powerful supernova explosions.

Red vs. blue supergiants

As a massive star evolves, it can change dramatically in size and temperature, sometimes moving between blue and red supergiant phases.
